RISK REGISTER
Identified risks with likelihood, impact and mitigation actions.
3 HIGH risks require immediate attention — Thierry overload, Jayron overload, and TGF Food having zero existing team.
Thierry carrying 4 roles across 2 silos
HIGHURGENT
Impact: Single point of failure for TGFbro and TGF Food production. If Thierry is unavailable, both silos stop producing.
Mitigation: Hire #2A (Second Video Editor) is the single most urgent hire. Must be resolved by Month 6 at latest.
Jayron covering Short-Form Editor across 4 silos
HIGH
Impact: Short-form content bottleneck as upload cadence increases. One illness or departure kills short-form across the group.
Mitigation: Viable short-term only. Each silo needs dedicated short-form editor by Month 6.
TGF Food has zero existing team
HIGH
Impact: Cannot produce any content until Hire #4 (Channel Manager) is secured. Every role in this silo is a hire.
Mitigation: Hire #4 is a hard prerequisite. Do not begin Food production without a Channel Manager in place.
Jay ADHD and spontaneous working habits
MEDIUM
Impact: Missed deadlines, inconsistent output, content schedule disruption.
Mitigation: Talent Manager (Abbey) owns Jay's schedule. Clear defined responsibilities. ADHD-friendly structured workflows. Reduce operational dependency on Jay over time.
Effort gap between Jay and Romell widening
MEDIUM
Impact: Team morale, talent relationship, potential public or contractual dispute.
Mitigation: Abbey's Talent Manager role includes explicit effort gap accountability framework. Romell given structured logistics and sponsorship responsibilities.
TGFbro comeback loses momentum after initial spike
MEDIUM
Impact: AdSense and sponsorship revenue stagnates. Justification for hiring plan weakens.
Mitigation: Content pipeline must maintain minimum 2 videos/month. Sponsorship deals reduce dependency on AdSense. New channels launching add ecosystem value.
Dan carrying CEO, Head of BD and Senior PM simultaneously
MEDIUM
Impact: Bandwidth constraints at senior level. Risk of strategic priorities being deprioritised for operational tasks.
Mitigation: Senior PM is the first central role to transition to a dedicated hire as revenue allows.
Key BBM team member departure during build phase
MEDIUM
Impact: Loss of institutional knowledge and operational capacity at a critical time.
Mitigation: All knowledge, processes and responsibilities documented in Claude Code knowledge base. No single point of failure in documentation.
Major sponsor pulling out mid-campaign
LOW
Impact: Revenue shortfall. Hiring plan delayed.
Mitigation: Never rely on a single sponsor deal. Diversify across categories. 3-month rolling pipeline of prospective deals maintained by Head of BD.
PR crisis involving Jay or Romell
LOW
Impact: Sponsor pullout, audience loss, brand damage.
Mitigation: Crisis communications plan to be developed. Talent Manager owns first response. Brand safety clauses in all sponsorship contracts.
